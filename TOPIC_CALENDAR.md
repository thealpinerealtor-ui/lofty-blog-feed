# Weekly Topic Rotation — Daily Posts

Each day of the week has a lane. Within a lane, the generator must pick a fresh angle not used in the last 8 weeks (check posts/ archive titles before writing).

| Day | Lane | Examples |
|-----|------|----------|
| Mon | **Local market update** | Kalispell/Whitefish weekly snapshot: inventory, median price, DOM, rate check. Source + month cited. Cody May / Leo Pareja style: data → what it means for you. |
| Tue | **Divorce home sales (niche)** | "Do we have to sell the house?", buyouts vs. selling, keeping kids in the school district, court timelines in Montana, dividing equity, one spouse won't cooperate. |
| Wed | **Buyer guides** | First-time buyers in Kalispell, VA loans (veteran angle — Ryan's lane), new construction vs. resale, winter buying, well/septic due diligence, land buying basics. |
| Thu | **Estate & probate sales (niche)** | Montana probate timeline, personal representative checklist, selling an inherited home from out of state, clearing out a family home, step-up basis basics (with CPA disclaimer). |
| Fri | **Community & lifestyle** | Neighborhood/area spotlights (amenities-focused, Fair-Housing safe), Whitefish Mountain season, Glacier NP access, Flathead Lake life, local events, moving-to-Montana guides. |
| Sat | **Seller guides** | Pricing strategy, prepping for market, timing the Flathead seasonal market, what kills deals in inspection, staging a mountain home. |
| Sun | **Brand/authority angle** | Mission-planning approach to transactions, negotiation lessons from high-pressure work, why discretion matters, fitness/discipline crossover pieces, client-scenario breakdowns (anonymized, hypothetical). |

## Rules
- 700–1,100 words per post.
- SEO title ≤ 60 chars where possible; H1 + 3-5 H2s; natural keyword use: "Kalispell homes for sale", "Whitefish real estate", "sell house divorce Montana", "probate real estate Flathead Valley", etc. — never stuffed.
- Monday posts REQUIRE fresh web research (current stats + mortgage rate). Other lanes: research when the topic is time-sensitive; evergreen otherwise.
- Every post: standard bio block from POSITIONING.md at the end.
- Every post: 1 soft CTA, Miner/Deol-style question-based close.
- Legal/tax topics: include disclaimer line.
